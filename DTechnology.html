DTechnology e-comerce

Description

E-comerce project developed with Django Framework.

You can browse the product catalog, as well as filter products by category, department or producer.

In order to buy a set of products, you will have to add them to the shopping cart and complete the order form.

If you want to pay with credit cart, Stripe platform will handle the process.

You can also track your orders by an order references that you will receive in your email once you have completed the payment process.
