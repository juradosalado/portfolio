Innoweb certification webside

Description

Webside to generate certifications for university events.

You can register for events, get scores participing in those event and track your scores in past events consulting your personal profile.

You can also download a PDF certificate with your score in a particular event, as well as the event details such as date or place of the event.

There is a podium with the result of scores from all the events registered in the database, which shows the top 3 users with higher scores.
