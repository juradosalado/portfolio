VidesSur Management Software

Description

Webside to manage the VidesSur's process, such as volunteering, stock and sponsorship.

You can register workers, childrens, godfathers, payments, volunteer and stock, among other NGO information. However, you must have an account to use the software, as it is for the exclusive use of the NGO.

All the information stored in the software can be updated and deleted, and you can link children to godfathers in order to manage differents sponsorships.
